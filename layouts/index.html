💡 This Week's Visibility Tip

Update your LinkedIn headline today. Most expert headlines say "Senior X at Company Y" - which tells organizers your employer, not your expertise. Rewrite it as: [What you help people do] + [Your specific domain]. Example: "Helping teams build ethical AI systems · Machine Learning & Responsible Tech." Do it now, before you forget.
